Webhook URL — three forms

click to expand

SignalBridge is bound to {{ host }}:{{ port }}. TradingView's servers can't reach 127.0.0.1 directly — for real alerts, expose it via ngrok or Cloudflare Tunnel and use the public HTTPS URL.

local 
http://127.0.0.1:{{ port }}/webhooks/tradingview
lan / tailscale 
http://HOST:{{ port }}/webhooks/tradingview
public tunnel (https) 
https://YOUR-TUNNEL-URL/webhooks/tradingview

Replace HOST with the machine's LAN or Tailscale magic-DNS name, and YOUR-TUNNEL-URL with the hostname your ngrok / Cloudflare Tunnel prints.
